VISA Information

Please check if you need a visa to enter Canada.

American citizens need to present a valid passport at the US-Canada border.
Note that this is a somewhat new regulation.

Visa Letters

If you require a letter for a visa application to travel to CGO, please send the following information to moshovos@eecg.toronto.edu

Note that we can only provide letters to (a) those who have paid their conference and/or workshop/tutorial registration fees, or (b) authors of papers that have been accepted to appear in the main conference or one of the affiliated workshops.

The information we need is the following:

  • Your name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Whether you want a faxed copy (if so, please provide a fax number).
  • Whether you need a hard copy of the letter sent and if so how quickly it needs to arrive (we will do our best, but please send requests to us early).
