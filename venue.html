Conference Venue


View Larger Map

The conference will be held at the Toronto Marriott Bloor Yorkville Hotel. The hotel is in the heart of downtown Toronto in the trendy Bloor-Yorkville neighbourhood. There are many restaruants and shops near the hotel. The Bloor metro/subway station is only one block away at the intersection of Bloor and Yonge. The subway station connects to the two main subway lines in Toronto, one going North/South and the other going East/West.
